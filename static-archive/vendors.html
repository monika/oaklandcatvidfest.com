Vendors

We are now accepting commercial, arts & craft and nonprofit vendor applications with payment for the 2014 festival!

Please fill out the below and submit payment via credit card here OR via check made out to "GWO, Inc." and mailed to GWO, Inc., 580 2nd Street, Oakland, CA 94607.

We have a limited number of vendor spaces available and expect to sell out early. Vendors are selected on a first come, first served basis and subject to festival approval. Vendor space is not secured until application is accepted and payment is processed. All vendors must have products or services for cats, art, technology or Oakland and are subject to festival approval. Refunds will be given to vendors who do not meet festival criteria.

Apply to Be a Vendor!

For more information on:

  • Commercial, nonprofit, or arts and crafts vendors, contact jay@greatwallofoakland.org
  • Selling food or beverages, contact miked@greatwallofoakland.org
